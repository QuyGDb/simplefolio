Projects

Graduation Project - Record Store (AR/MR)

An AR app that lets you place and explore a virtual vinyl shop in your real space. Browse three music genres—Pop, Rap, Rock—via wall-mounted artworks that play videos and show text histories. Interact with virtual records: rotate, zoom, swipe tracks, and hear spatialized audio. Select “Bohemian Rhapsody” to unlock a moving portal into a Live Aid 1985 concert scene.
